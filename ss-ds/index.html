Multi-brand design system

Product

White labeled design system that support 50+ design files with different brandings.

Role

Design system lead

Overview

Simplestream Open link is a B2B OTT service provider. We design and build apps across all platforms including mobile, tablet, web, TV for clients across the globe. As a small team of 2, we need a relable way to efficiently switch between different clients' brandings and generate designs needed for production or sales proposals.

Problem

We leveraged a third-party Figma plugin to save colors, fonts, and images as reusable themes, aimed to enable quick replacement of selected designs with pre-saved assets. However, due to the large number of screens in our projects, the plugin often took several minutes to process. This frequently caused Figma to freeze or glitch, leading to errors that required time-consuming manual checks and fixes.

Goal

Refactored our design system to adopt Figma’s newly introduced native “Swap Library” feature. This improved reliability, eliminated glitches and errors caused by the plugin, and reduced the time needed to switch between brand themes to just a few seconds.

Design system refractor

In order to adopt the Figma swap library feature, I defined a new sructure and rename every token or layers of the following:

Colour tokens

Our colour token template contains a wide range of component-specific color tokens, giving our clients greater flexibility to customize the look and feel of their apps.

In order for these tokens to be compatible with the Figma swap library feature, I defined a new sructure and rename every token.

Imagery

We also created a template for the 16:9 video thumbnails used throughout the app. We would replace these placeholder images with content sourced from clients’ websites, sometimes for production-ready designs, other times for sales proposals.

Typeface

Font styles are fixed in size and weight to ensure text remains visible and the app stays accessible. However, we give clients the flexibility to choose a single typeface to be used consistently across all their apps.

Components

All the components and UI design templates are linked with the template theme files ready for swap library.

Swap library

Every time we onboard a new client, we duplicate our templates and set up a custom theme. By swapping the design library, we can instantly apply the client’s unique look and feel. This approach has allowed my team of 2 designers to efficiently manage and deliver a large library of client designs with ease.
